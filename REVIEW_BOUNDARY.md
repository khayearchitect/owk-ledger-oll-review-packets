# Review Boundary

This repository exists for public format critique of a synthetic reviewer-ready packet.

## Safe Review Scope

Reviewers may comment on:

- missing fields
- unclear sections
- unnecessary sections
- useful confidence markers
- packet structure
- CSV usefulness
- whether the packet could reduce back-and-forth before review

## Out Of Scope

Do not provide or request:

- tax advice
- legal advice
- accounting advice
- payroll advice
- audit conclusions
- compliance certification
- business valuation
- investment recommendation
- real-client data
- real bank data
- real ID numbers
- real employee records

## Core Notice

Prepared by the business. Not professionally reviewed unless marked by a verified reviewer.
